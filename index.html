About Me

I am an Indiana native. While in high school I attended J. Everett Light Career Center and enrolled in the Automotive Technology program to pursue a career as an Automotive Technician. I was able to gain part time employment as an Automotive Technician during the program. After high school I attended Ivy Tech Community College and graduated with a certificate in Automotive Electrical and Electronics. After working as an Automotive Technician for the past 10 years, I decided to pursue a career in Web Development. I attended Eleven Fifty Academy in the summer of 2020 to gain a broader knowledge and understanding of Web Development. I hope to have a successful career in the tech Industry.
